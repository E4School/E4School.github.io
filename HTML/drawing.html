Drawing

If you have ever felt the urge to pick up your pencil and draw, then why not try it now?

Before you start, please take a moment to make sure you have the correct tools. You will need:

  • Pencil
  • Paper
  • Eraser

The first thing you will learn how to draw is an octopus.

  1. The first thing you need to do is draw a semicircle. Make sure the opening of this shape is at the bottom of your page.
  2. Next, draw a long bean-like shape starting from the left bottom opening of the cirle. It should resemble an octopus leg.
  3. Next, repeat step #2 on the right side. The figure should now look mirrored.
  4. The next step is to add another long bean-shaped leg next to the one on the far left.
  5. Repeat the previous step, adding a leg to the middle of the bottom opening. Make sure to move it further down than the other legs to ensure a good look.
  6. Next, repeat the previous steps again, closing up the gap that is left.
  7. The next step is to draw 2 dots in the middle of the circle for eyes.
  8. Next, draw a smile on the octopus, below the eyes. Make sure it is centered!
  9. The next step is to add 2 small bubbles under each tentacle. These should give the octopus a more realistic look.
  10. Lastly, color in the octopus, and you're done!
